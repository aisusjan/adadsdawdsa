Abai Qunanbaiuly

About Abai

Abai Qunanbaiuly (1845–1904) was a great Kazakh poet, philosopher, composer, and translator. He shaped a new era of Kazakh literature and inspired the nation to pursue knowledge and education. His works had a profound impact on the cultural and spiritual development of the Kazakh people.

Important Events

  1. Born in 1845 in the Chinghiz mountains.
  2. In the 1870s, translated works of Russian classics into Kazakh.
  3. In the 1880s–1890s, wrote his main poems and prose works.
  4. Passed away in 1904.

Main Contributions

  • "Words of Edification" (Қара сөздер)
  • Collections of poetry
  • Translations of Pushkin, Lermontov, Krylov into Kazakh
